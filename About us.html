About us

Gema Restaurant: This is a dream come true. The name was founded and created by Miss Gema hence the name GEMA restaurant. The restaurant is a fast food restaurant.
We are located within the bustling city of Nairobi. We are a fast paced restaurant and we do our best to ensure fast and great customer services.

Our burgers are accompanied by a variety of fries. We also offer table bookings for various events such as family meetings, chamas, private business deals and any other meet-ups that need a secured environment.The restaurant opens at 0630hrs to 2230hrs. Our outdoor catering department caters for all kind of functions and events.

As you plan for your dream wedding, meet our banquets and events service team for a tailored package that meets your taste. We arrange for both indoor and outdoor venues. You can always reach out for these services.
